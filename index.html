Biography

Mohaimen A. Syed. Student at the University of Southern California majoring in Computer Science, currently a Sophomore. Born in Dhaka, Bangladesh, grew up playing and watching sports, reading books, playing video games and with an innate love for technology. Moved to Queens, New York in 2018 after high-school, studied at LaGuardia Community College for a year while also working as a Sales Intern at Wingsteck Inc. working as an aircraft parts Broker, and later got a job as a Part-time Stylist at Scotch and Soda. Studied at Rutgers University, New Brunswick for a semester and finally transferred to USC. Aspire to be a software developer integrating software with machine learning and AI.
